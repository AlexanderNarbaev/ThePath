Welcome

The Spiral of Consciousness is an open, evolving system of practices, ethics, and worldview, created for navigation in the era of the Great Trial (21st–23rd centuries).

It does not demand you abandon your beliefs, does not impose dogma, and does not promise easy answers. Instead, it offers proven tools for resilience, cooperation, and long-term flourishing — personal, communal, and civilizational.

Three Prohibitions

Do not kill. Do not torture. Do not enslave. — absolute red lines for the individual, community, and state.

Four Principles

Develop yourself. Create and help. Think long-term. Preserve and multiply consciousness.
