# Guide Map — Spiral of Consciousness

## Welcome

The Spiral of Consciousness is an open, evolving system of practices, ethics, and worldview, created for navigation in the era of the Great Trial (21st–23rd centuries). It does not demand you abandon your beliefs, does not impose dogma, and does not promise easy answers. Instead, it offers proven tools for resilience, cooperation, and long-term flourishing — personal, communal, and civilizational.

The Spiral is a supra-state moral compass. It does not serve any state, but sets ethical boundaries for all of them.

This text is a guide map to the system. Choose your path based on who you are and how much time you are ready to dedicate.

## Study Paths

### Quick Start (for everyone — ~2 hours)
1. **Manifesto** — read to understand the spirit of the system. 10 min.
2. **Module 0 (Canon)** — read and accept the axioms, prohibitions, principles. 30 min.
3. **Module 3 (Practices)** — master daily 3 minutes and simple rituals. 60 min.
4. **Module 5 (Conflicts)** — learn how the Reconciliation Circle works. 30 min.

### For Mentors (~15–20 hours)
**Required:** Modules 0, 2, 3, 4, 5, 9, 10. **Recommended:** Modules 1, 8.

### For Community Coordinators (~25–30 hours)
All from the Mentor path, plus Modules 7, 8, and 4 (in-depth).

### For Statesmen (~30–40 hours)
**Required:** Modules 0, 2, 5, 6, 8, 13. **Recommended:** Modules 7, 10, 11.

### Deep Study (self-paced)
Modules 1, 6, 11, 12.
