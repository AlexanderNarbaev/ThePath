# Manifesto of the Spiral of Consciousness

## A Moral Compass for the Individual, the Citizen, and Authority

---

### The Era of the Great Trial

We live in a time when forces of degradation attack the very foundation of humanity.

Digital opium steals our attention. Blind economy exhausts the planet. The black box of AI learns to think for us. Toxic distrust divides people who could become allies.

This is not chaos. It is a **filter**. Those who retain depth of consciousness, cooperation, and long-term thinking will pass through it. The rest will remain in agony or dissolve into gray boredom.

We need a new path. Not religion — it has too much dogma. Not ideology — it too often justifies violence. Simply a practical system for survival and flourishing.

**The Spiral of Consciousness is this path.**

---

### Three Prohibitions: Red Lines for All

These prohibitions are absolute. No goal, no benefit, no order can override them.

**Do not kill.** Exception — only a direct, imminent, proven threat to life. Death penalty, extrajudicial executions, aggressive war — these are murder. They are forbidden.

**Do not torture.** No torture. Under no circumstances. Not to save lives, not for information, not for punishment. This is an absolute prohibition without exception.

**Do not enslave.** No forced labor, debt bondage, human trafficking. No deprivation of autonomy without voluntary consent.

---

### Four Principles of Flourishing

**Develop yourself.** Learn, train attention, care for body and mind.

**Create and help.** Add value to the world. Art, science, craft, education — all contributions to the common cause.

**Think long-term.** Your choice today affects life in 100, 1000, 1,000,000 years.

**Preserve and multiply consciousness.** Guard attention as the greatest treasure. Digital noise, infinite feeds, clip thinking — these are enemies.

---

### Practice: 3 Minutes a Day

**Morning:** "Today I choose the ascending spiral."
**Before screens:** "Why am I opening this? Is this a conscious choice or escape from silence?"
**Evening:** "What did I do to preserve consciousness? Where did I yield to entropy?"

No obligations. No fees. No faith. Only choice.

---

### The Call

Start with three minutes today.
Find or create a community — online or offline.
Choose the ascending spiral — every day.

> *You are a conscious node. Entropy pulls downward. Choice pulls upward. Choose the ascending spiral.*
