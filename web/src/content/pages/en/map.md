## Welcome

The "Spiral of Consciousness" is an open, evolving system of practices, ethics, and worldview, created for navigation in the era of the Great Trial (XXI–XXIII centuries). It does not demand that you abandon your convictions, imposes no dogmas, and promises no easy answers. Instead, it offers proven tools for resilience, cooperation, and long-term flourishing — personal, communal, and civilizational.

The Spiral is a supranational moral guide. It serves no state, but sets ethical boundaries for any. It acknowledges the reality of armies, police, borders, and sovereignty, but demands their ethical limitation by the three prohibitions and four principles. For those who serve the state, the Spiral offers a clear compass: how to preserve honor without becoming complicit in crimes.

This text is a guide map to the system. Choose your path depending on who you are and how much time you are ready to dedicate.

## 🧭 Study Paths

### ⚡ Quick Start (for everyone — ≈2 hours)
Get acquainted with the core and fundamental practices to start applying them in your life.

1. **Manifesto of the Spiral of Consciousness** — read to understand the spirit of the system. 10 min.
2. **Module 0 (Canon)** — read and accept the axioms, prohibitions, principles, and the section "The Spiral and the State." 30 min.
3. **Module 3 (Practices)** — master the daily 3 minutes and simple rituals. 60 min.
4. **Module 5 (Conflicts)** — learn how the Circle of Reconciliation works. 30 min.

After this, you will be able to participate in Assemblies of Reason, Civic Rituals, and apply the basic practices on your own.

### 🧭 For Mentors (≈15–20 hours)
A deep understanding of the system and facilitation skills.

- **Required:** Module 0, Module 2 (ethics, especially the section "Ethics of Power and Force"), Module 3, Module 4 (resilience, "Digital Fortress," community under a repressive state), Module 5 (conflicts, Facilitator's Code, appeals mechanism), Module 9 (psychology, especially trauma work and collective trauma), Module 10 (education).
- **Recommended:** Module 1 (cosmology), Module 8 (politics, especially sociocracy, community schism).
- **Optional:** Module 6, 7, 11, 12, 14.

After study and practical training, you can undergo mentor certification. If you plan to work in the army, police, or emergency services, additionally complete the specialization "Mentor in State Institutions" (Module 10, Section 3).

### 🏛️ For Community Coordinators (≈25–30 hours)
Community management, economics, and external relations.

- **Everything from the mentor path.**
- **Plus:** Module 7 (economics, cooperatives, long-term funds, connection to reserves), Module 8 (politics, civic assemblies, procedure for schism), Module 4 (in depth: civic mutual aid in emergencies).

### 🏛️ For State Officials (politicians, military, judges, diplomats — ≈30–40 hours)
Applying the principles of the Spiral in public service and lawmaking.

- **Required:** Module 0 (especially the section "The Spiral and the State"), Module 2 (ethics of power and force, the "dirty hands" dilemma, criminal order), Module 5 (interaction with the state judicial system), Module 6 (AI in the military sphere, digital sovereignty, certification checklist), Module 8 (the fourth ethical power, civic assemblies, subsidiarity), Module 13 (Ethical State, defense and security, roadmap of reforms).
- **Recommended:** Module 7 (sovereign funds, 30-hour week, tax transparency), Module 10 (specialization for state institutions), Module 11 (Council of Civilizations, protocols for interstate synthesis), Module 12 (military ethics, international law).
- **Optional:** Module 1, Module 9 (combatant trauma, psychology for professionals), Module 14 (national regenerative economy).

### 🔭 In-Depth Study (for enthusiasts and researchers)
Understanding the philosophical foundations, technological challenges, and global synthesis.

- **Module 1** (cosmology) — for immersion in myth and meaning.
- **Module 6** (technology and ethics) — for answers to the challenges of AI and biohacking.
- **Module 11** (global synthesis) — how to unite different cultural traditions.
- **Module 12** (library) — for independent study of primary sources.

## 🤝 How to Contribute

The "Spiral of Consciousness" is a living system, and we welcome community participation.

- **Become a mentor** — follow the "Mentor" path and apply the practices in your life.
- **Create a local community** — use Module 5 for conflict resolution and Module 8 for governance.
- **Implement Spiral principles in public service** — use the "State Official" path.
