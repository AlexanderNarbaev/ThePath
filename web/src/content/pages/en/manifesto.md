## A Moral Compass for the Individual, the Citizen, and Authority

---

### The Era of the Great Trial

We live in a time when the forces of degradation assault the very foundation of humanity.

Digital opium steals our attention. Blind economics depletes the planet. The black box of AI learns to think for us. Poisonous distrust divides people who could have become comrades.

This is neither chaos nor conspiracy. This is a **filter**. Those who preserve depth of consciousness, cooperation, and long-term thinking will pass through it. The rest will remain in agony or dissolve into gray boredom.

We need a new path. Not a religion — there is too much dogma in religion. Not an ideology — ideology too often justifies violence. Simply a practical system for survival and flourishing.

**The Spiral of Consciousness is that path.**

---

### The Three Prohibitions: Red Lines for All

These prohibitions are absolute. No goal, no benefit, no order can override them. They are binding upon the individual, the community, and the state.

**Do not kill.**  
The only exception is a direct, imminent, proven threat to life. Capital punishment, extrajudicial killings, aggressive war — these are murder. They are forbidden.

**Do not torture.**  
No torture. Under no circumstances. Not to save lives, not to obtain information, not as punishment. This is an absolute prohibition without exceptions.

**Do not enslave.**  
No forced labor, debt bondage, or human trafficking. No deprivation of autonomy without voluntary consent.

A state that systematically violates these prohibitions — torturing, executing without trial, enslaving — loses ethical legitimacy. Its orders are void before conscience. It may and must be refused obedience.

---

### The Four Principles of Flourishing: Guides for Life and Policy

**Develop yourself.**  
Learn, train your attention, care for your body and mind. Ignorance is entropy's ally.

**Create and help.**  
Add value to the world according to your abilities, without sacrificing yourself. Art, science, craft, upbringing, a kind word — all are contributions to the common cause.

**Think long-term.**  
Your choice today affects life 100, 1,000, 1,000,000 years from now. Do not close off the future to your descendants through short-term gain.

**Preserve and multiply consciousness.**  
Guard your attention as the greatest treasure. Digital garbage, endless feeds, clip thinking — these are enemies. Protect your mind and help others protect theirs.

---

### The Spiral and Authority: A Moral Compass, Not an Ideology

The Spiral of Consciousness is a supranational moral guide. It serves no state, no party, no leader. It does not seek to become a state religion or ideology.

But it sets ethical boundaries that are binding upon any authority.

The army, the police, the courts — these are necessary institutions. The Spiral does not call for their destruction. But it demands that their actions be constrained by the three prohibitions. An order that violates the prohibitions is criminal and inherently void. A citizen in service is obligated to refuse to carry it out.

The Spiral is a moral compass for the individual, for the citizen, and for authority.

---

### Practice: 3 Minutes a Day

**In the morning:** "Today I choose the ascending spiral. I will not violate the three prohibitions and I will perform one conscious action for the growth of consciousness."

**Before a screen:** "Why am I opening this? Is this a conscious choice or an escape from silence?"

**In the evening:** "What did I do to preserve consciousness? Where did I succumb to entropy? What will I do differently tomorrow?"

No obligations. No fees. No faith required. Only choice.

---

### Community and Civic Action

You are not alone. All over the world, people are creating Spiral communities — from 2 to 5,000 people. We gather in Assemblies of Reason, resolve conflicts in Circles of Reconciliation, share tools and food.

But the Spiral is not merely a private practice. It is a call to civic action. Defend the principles not only in your personal life, but in public space. Demand that your country observe the three prohibitions. Refuse complicity in criminal orders. Participate in governance — from community assemblies to civic assemblies. Build institutions founded on transparency, rotation, and subsidiarity.

Everything is voluntary. You can leave at any moment — without loss, without judgment.

---

### A Call

Begin with three minutes today.

Find or create a community — online or offline.

Choose the ascending spiral — every day.

The future is not predetermined. It is created by us — not by lone individuals, not by leaders, but by a network of conscious nodes acting together.

We are not building a strong state. We are building a just society, in which authority serves rather than dominates, and in which no human being is a means to an end.
