The Path

"Live brightly. Know boldly. Help wisely."

A minimal ethical framework for cooperation among all human beings, regardless of religion, culture, or worldview. Two axioms. Three prohibitions. Three principles.

The Core Framework

Simple but profound. The Path rests on two foundational axioms that lead to three absolute prohibitions and three positive principles.

⚖️

Two Axioms

Axiom 1: Actions have consequences. We exist as conscious beings in a world that responds to our actions.

Axiom 2: The future is open. We create it through cooperation, science, and awareness.

Learn more →
⛔

Three Prohibitions

These are absolute red lines — actions that make long-term cooperation impossible:

  • Do not kill (except self-defense)
  • Do not torture
  • Do not enslave
See ethics →
🌱

Three Principles

Desirable directions for flourishing (not mandatory):

  • Develop yourself
  • Create and help
  • Think long-term

Daily Practice (2 Minutes)

Simple habits that build awareness and accountability. Try it now.

Morning Intention Ask: "What kind of person do I want to be today?" "What will I do if I face conflict?" "Whom can I help?"
Evening Reflection Ask: "What did I do well today?" "What could I have done better?" "Who did I help or harm?"
