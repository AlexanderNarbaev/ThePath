Quick Start Guide

30-Day Starter Plan for Individuals and Groups

Home › Quick Start

How This Guide Works

This guide is a suggestion, not a prescription. You may skip days, repeat days, or extend the 30 days as needed. There is no pressure to perform perfectly — only to explore thoughtfully.

No Prerequisites: No cost. No membership required. No background required. Just practice. Just begin.

Interactive 30-Day Tracker

Click each day to mark it complete. Progress is saved in your browser.

Days 1–7: Foundation

  • Day 1: Read Document 1 (One-Page Summary).
  • Day 2: Practice morning intention.
  • Day 3: Practice morning intention + evening reflection.
  • Day 4: Reflect: What barriers do I face?
  • Day 5: Practice both twice today.
  • Day 6: Share the summary with a friend.
  • Day 7: Review the week. What worked? (Rest day optional)

Weekly reflection: What felt natural? What felt forced?

Tip: If you miss a day, just continue — no guilt. Restart without judgment.

Days 8–14: Integration

  • Day 8: Read Document 2 (Axioms).
  • Day 9: Practice both.
  • Day 10: Consider: When do the prohibitions matter?
  • Day 11: Practice both.
  • Day 12: Read Document 12 (Stories). Pick one.
  • Day 13: Practice both.
  • Day 14: Review. (Rest day optional)

Weekly reflection: Which of the three principles resonates most with me right now?

Days 15–21: Connection

  • Day 15: Read Document 5 (Community Charter).
  • Day 16: Find or form a small community.
  • Day 17: Visit an existing community (online or in person).
  • Day 18: Practice both.
  • Day 19: Suggest an Assembly of Reason.
  • Day 20: Practice both.
  • Day 21: Review month 1. (Rest day optional)

Days 22–30: Growth

  • Day 22: Read Document 4 (Practices, Level 1).
  • Day 23: Add weekly practice.
  • Day 24: Practice both daily + weekly.
  • Day 25: Read Document 14 (Festivals).
  • Day 26: Consider attending a festival.
  • Day 27: Read any remaining document.
  • Day 28: Practice both daily + weekly.
  • Day 29: Reflect: What's next?
  • Day 30: Share with another friend. Welcome to the Path.

For Groups

If you're facilitating a group:

  • Meet weekly (e.g., every Day 7, 14, 21, 28) to share experiences.
  • Use the Assembly of Reason format — each person speaks without interruption.
  • Pair up members for mutual accountability.
  • End-of-month celebration: Day 30 is a natural time to mark your journey together.

What After 30 Days?

Congratulations on completing the starter plan. Here are some next steps:

  • Continue the daily practice (morning intention + evening reflection)
  • Join or form a local community
  • Explore other documents
  • Share the guide with a friend
  • Consider attending a festival
