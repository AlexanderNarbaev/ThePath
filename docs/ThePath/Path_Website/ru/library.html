Search & Filter

Philosophy

Classical Philosophy

Republic — Plato. Classic exploration of justice, the ideal state, and the role of the philosopher-king.
Nicomachean Ethics — Aristotle. Foundations of virtue ethics, the concept of eudaimonia (flourishing).
Meditations — Marcus Aurelius. Stoic philosophy in practice, daily reflections on duty and virtue.

Modern Philosophy

Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals — Kant. The categorical imperative: treat humanity as an end, never merely as a means.
On Liberty — Mill. Freedom of speech, self-sovereignty, harm principle.

Ethics & Cooperation

The Evolution of Cooperation — Axelrod. How cooperation emerges in iterated prisoner's dilemma.
Governing the Commons — Ostrom. Collective management of shared resources.
The Righteous Mind — Haidt. Moral psychology, intuition and reasoning.

Politics

A Theory of Justice — Rawls. Justice as fairness, original position.
The Power of the Powerless — Havel. Dissidence, civil society, living in truth.

Economics

Doughnut Economics — Raworth. Sustainable economics.
Development as Freedom — Sen. Freedom as development.

Psychology

Thinking, Fast and Slow — Kahneman. Cognitive biases, dual-process theory.
The Lucifer Effect — Zimbardo. Situational forces and evil.

Religion & Spirituality

Tao Te Ching — Lao Tzu. Wu wei (non-action), natural order.
Dhammapada — Buddha. Buddhist ethics, Middle Path.

Science Fiction

The Dispossessed — Le Guin. Anarchism and cooperation.
Mars Trilogy — Robinson. Long-term terraforming.

Modern Thinkers

Superintelligence — Bostrom. AI risk.
What We Owe the Future — MacAskill. Longtermism.
The Precipice — Ord. Existential risk.
Note: This library is a starting point, not an endorsement. Inclusion does not mean endorsement of every idea in a book. The Path encourages reading broadly and thinking critically.
