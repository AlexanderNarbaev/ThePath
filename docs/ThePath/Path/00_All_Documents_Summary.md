## Summary

**The Path — Evolutionary Framework of Flourishing (Version 12.1)** is a minimal ethical framework (two pragmatic axioms, three prohibitions, three principles) designed to enable cooperation across all human diversity — adaptable to any culture, religion, or political system — without requiring abandonment of existing beliefs, providing practices from 2-minute daily rituals to yearly festivals, with the goal of long-term flourishing of civilization.
