## The Path — Evolutionary Framework of Flourishing

### 30-Day Starter Plan

This guide is for individuals or small groups (2–10) ready to begin exploring the Path.

---

## How This Guide Works

This guide is a suggestion, not a prescription. You may skip days, repeat days, or extend the 30 days as needed. There is no pressure to perform perfectly—only to explore thoughtfully. If something doesn't work for you, adjust it. The Path is flexible; there is no wrong way to begin.

### Days 1–7: Foundation

- **Day 1:** Read [Document 1] (One-Page Summary).
- **Day 2:** Practice morning intention.
- **Day 3:** Practice morning intention + evening reflection.
- **Day 4:** Reflect: What barriers do I face?
- **Day 5:** Practice both twice today.
- **Day 6:** Share the summary with a friend.
- **Day 7:** Review the week. What worked? | **Rest day (optional)**

**Weekly reflection prompt:** What felt natural? What felt forced?

**Troubleshooting tip:** If you miss a day, just continue—no guilt. Missing a day is normal. Restart without judgment.

### Days 8–14: Integration

- **Day 8:** Read [Document 2] (Axioms).
- **Day 9:** Practice both.
- **Day 10:** Consider: When do the prohibitions matter?
- **Day 11:** Practice both.

## For Groups: Adapting the 30-Day Plan

If you're facilitating a group, consider these suggestions:

- **Meet weekly** (e.g., every Day 7, 14, 21, 28) to share experiences and reflect together.
- **Use the Assembly of Reason format** (see Document 04) for group meetings—each person speaks without interruption, and listening is as valued as speaking.
- **Pair up members** for mutual accountability. Partners can check in briefly each day or share a weekly reflection.
- **End-of-month celebration:** Day 30 is a natural time to mark your group's journey together.

## What After 30 Days?

Congratulations on completing the starter plan. Here are some next steps:

- **Continue the daily practice** (morning intention + evening reflection) — this is the foundation.
- **Join or form a local community** — connection with others strengthens the journey.
- **Explore other documents** — Document 04 for more practices, Document 12 for stories, Document 17 for FAQs.
- **Share the guide with a friend** — the Path grows by being shared.
- **Consider attending a festival** (Document 14) — celebrate with the broader community.
