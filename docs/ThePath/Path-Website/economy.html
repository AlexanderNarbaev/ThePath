Economy

Three-tier economic model, voluntary funding, and ethical economics

Three-Tier Model

Tier 1: Survival

Basic needs: food, shelter, healthcare, education. Provided through mutual aid and community support.

Tier 2: Development

Opportunity for growth: skills, education,创业. Earned through contribution and voluntary exchange.

Tier 3: Abundance

Extraordinary achievement and legacy. Rewards for exceptional contribution to human flourishing.

Voluntary Funding

  • No mandatory dues: All contributions are voluntary
  • Transparency: All spending is visible to members
  • Direct impact: Know where your money goes
  • Mutual aid: Support each other in need

Ethical Economics

  • No investments in companies violating the three prohibitions
  • Fair wages and safe working conditions
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Transparent pricing
