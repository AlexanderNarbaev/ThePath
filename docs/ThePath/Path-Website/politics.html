Politics & Governance

Political principles, subsidiarity, and separation of powers

Core Principles

  • Subsidiarity: Decisions made at the lowest effective level
  • Separation of powers: No concentration of authority
  • Right to exit: Anyone can leave any political arrangement
  • Transparency: All governance is visible

Long-Term Council

A council focused on long-term consequences, representing future generations. Considers 10, 50, 100-year impacts of decisions.

No Permanent Central Authority

The Path does not seek to govern. It provides a framework for cooperation that works within existing systems.
