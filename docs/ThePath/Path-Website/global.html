Global Coordination

Ad hoc councils, networks, and planetary cooperation

Ad Hoc Councils

For planetary challenges (climate, AI, pandemics), temporary councils form to address specific issues. No permanent central authority.

Networks

  • Communities voluntarily join specific projects
  • Every project is opt-in
  • All coordination is transparent
  • No强制 membership

No Permanent Central Authority

The Path does not have a headquarters, leadership, or hierarchy. Coordination happens through voluntary cooperation and transparent communication.
