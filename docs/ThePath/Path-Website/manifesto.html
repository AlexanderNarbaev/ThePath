Manifesto for Adoption

Why we need a minimal ethical framework

We live in a world of unprecedented complexity. Global challenges — climate change, AI, pandemics — require cooperation across every boundary. Yet our existing frameworks divide us.

The Path offers what no religion, ideology, or nation can: a minimal floor of agreement that anyone can accept. Not because we agree on everything, but because we agree on what matters most.

We exist. Our actions have consequences. The future is open.

Do not kill. Do not torture. Do not enslave.

Develop yourself. Create and help. Think long-term.

This is not a utopia. It is a starting point. Join us in creating a better future.
