Practices & Rituals

Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly practices that develop awareness and empathy

Daily Practice — 2 Minutes

Today's Practice

Morning Intention Ask yourself: "What kind of person do I want to be today?"
Evening Reflection Ask yourself: "What did I do well today?"

Morning Intention (1 minute)

  1. Pause and take one deep breath
  2. Ask yourself: "What kind of person do I want to be today?"
  3. Ask yourself: "What will I do if I face anger / fear / conflict?"
  4. Ask yourself: "Whom can I help today?"
  5. Set your intention for the day

Evening Reflection (1 minute)

  1. Pause and take one deep breath
  2. Ask yourself: "What did I do well today?"
  3. Ask yourself: "What could I have done better?"
  4. Ask yourself: "Who did I help or harm today?"
  5. Let go of the day and rest

Weekly Practice

Assembly of Reason

Meet weekly with your community (in person or virtual) to:

  • Share successes and challenges
  • Discuss ethical dilemmas
  • Make decisions together
  • Support each other

Review the Week

Each week, reflect on:

  • Did I act according to my values?
  • Where did I fall short?
  • What can I improve?
  • Who needs my help?

Monthly Practices

  • Deep Reflection: Take one hour to review your month's actions and growth
  • Skill Development: Learn something new that helps you create value
  • Connection: Reach out to someone you haven't spoken to in a while

Yearly Practices

  • Yearly Review: Reflect on the past year — growth, lessons, relationships
  • Set Intentions: Define your direction for the coming year
  • Celebrate: Participate in the four festivals of the Path
