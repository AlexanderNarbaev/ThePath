Education

Teaching without indoctrination

Age-Appropriate Practices

Children (0-12)

Focus on basic empathy, sharing, and simple ethical concepts through stories and play.

Adolescents (12-18)

Introduce the axioms and prohibitions. Encourage critical thinking and discussion.

Adults (18+)

Full engagement with all documents. Emphasis on practice and community.

Teaching Without Indoctrination

  • Present the Path as one option among many
  • Encourage questioning and critical thinking
  • Do not force acceptance
  • Model the principles, don't just talk about them
  • Respect different viewpoints
