30-Day Quick Start Guide

Practice the Path daily and transform your life

How It Works

Each day, complete the practice and click the day number to mark it complete. Progress is saved automatically.

Your 30-Day Journey

Day 1: Read the One-Page Summary

Start by understanding the basic framework. Read the summary in full.

Practice: After reading, write down: What is one thing you agree with?

Day 2: Understand Axiom One

Actions have consequences. Think of a time when your action had an unexpected consequence.

Practice: Ask yourself: "What will happen if I do X?" before your next action today.

Day 3: Understand Axiom Two

The future is open. We create it through cooperation, science, and awareness.

Practice: Plan one thing you will do tomorrow. Believe it can matter.

Day 4: The First Prohibition

Do not kill intentionally (except direct, imminent, proven self-defense).

Practice: Reflect on non-violent conflict resolution. How would you handle anger without violence?

Day 5: The Second Prohibition

Do not torture — no exceptions.

Practice: Think about power dynamics. How do you use power without harming others?

Day 6: The Third Prohibition

Do not enslave — no forced labor, debt bondage, or human trafficking.

Practice: Consider your work. Are you free to leave? Do you allow others freedom?

Day 7: Principle One — Develop Yourself

Continuously increase your awareness, empathy, and responsibility.

Practice: Learn something new today. Any topic, any skill.

Day 8: Principle Two — Create and Help

Add value to the world. Make things, solve problems, support others.

Practice: Do one thing today that creates value for someone else.

Day 9: Principle Three — Think Long-Term

Consider the consequences of your actions for future generations.

Practice: Think about something you're working on. Will it matter in 10 years?

Day 10: Start Daily Practice

Begin the morning intention and evening reflection.

Practice: Do your first morning intention and evening reflection.

Day 11: Self-Reflection

Review your first 10 days. What's working? What's not?

Practice: Write down 3 things you learned about yourself.

Day 12: Empathy Practice

Put yourself in someone else's shoes today.

Practice: Before judging someone today, ask: "What might it be like to be them?"

Day 13: Mindful Communication

Pay attention to how you communicate.

Practice: Listen more than you speak today. Notice what you learn.

Day 14: Gratitude

Notice what you're grateful for.

Practice: Write down 3 things you're grateful for today.

Day 15: Review Midpoint

You're halfway there! Reflect on your journey.

Practice: What habits have you formed? What needs more work?

Day 16: Handle Difficulty

When things go wrong, remember the axioms.

Practice: The next time you face difficulty, ask: "What are the consequences of my response?"

Day 17: Nature and Growth

The spiral represents growth without closure.

Practice: Spend time in nature. Observe continuous growth.

Day 18: Relationships

Consider your relationships through the Path lens.

Practice: Reach out to someone you haven't spoken to in a while.

Day 19: Work and Purpose

Think about your work through the three principles.

Practice: Does your work develop you? Create value? Matter long-term?

Day 20: Community

Consider joining or forming a community.

Practice: Look for local groups that share similar values.

Day 21: Forgiveness

Let go of past harms. Holding onto anger harms you more than others.

Practice: Identify one thing you're holding onto. Consider letting it go.

Day 22: Patience

Long-term thinking requires patience.

Practice: Do something today that has a delayed benefit.

Day 23: Curiosity

Science is an engine of progress through systematic inquiry.

Practice: Learn something you disagree with. Understand before judging.

Day 24: Integrity

Align your actions with your values.

Practice: Where are you not being consistent? What holds you back?

Day 25: Celebration

Recognize progress. Celebrate wins.

Practice: Acknowledge how far you've come in 25 days.

Day 26: Service

Look for ways to help beyond yourself.

Practice: Do something kind for a stranger today.

Day 27: Boundaries

Protect your ability to help by maintaining healthy boundaries.

Practice: Say no to something that doesn't align with your values.

Day 28: Future Vision

What future are you creating?

Practice: Describe the world you want to help create.

Day 29: Integration

Bring everything together.

Practice: Write a summary of what the Path means to you now.

Day 30: Continue the Journey

Congratulations! The Path is now a part of your life.

Practice: Decide: How will you continue? What comes next?

Remember: This is just the beginning. The spiral continues to grow.
