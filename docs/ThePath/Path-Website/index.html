The Core Framework

1Axiom One

We exist as conscious beings in a world where our actions have consequences.

Our choices matter. What we do affects ourselves and others. This is the foundation of ethics.

2Axiom Two

The future is open and is created by us through cooperation, science, and awareness.

We shape what comes next. Together, we can build a better future for all.

1Prohibition

Do not kill intentionally (except direct, imminent, proven self-defense).

2Prohibition

Do not torture — no exceptions.

3Prohibition

Do not enslave — no forced labor, debt bondage, or human trafficking.

1Principle

Develop yourself — increase your awareness, empathy, and responsibility.

2Principle

Create and help — add value to the world, support others.

3Principle

Think for the long-term future — consider consequences for generations to come.
