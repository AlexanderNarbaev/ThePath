Festivals & Calendar

Four yearly celebrations aligned with the seasons

Spring Equinox Festival

Celebration of renewal, new beginnings, and the awakening of nature. A time for setting intentions.

Summer Solstice Festival

June 21

Celebration of abundance, creativity, and the height of light. A time for gratitude and joy.

Autumn Equinox Festival

September 22/23

Celebration of harvest, reflection, and sharing. A time for thanksgiving and preparation.

Winter Solstice Festival

December 21

Celebration of rest, darkness, and the return of light. A time for inner reflection and hope.
