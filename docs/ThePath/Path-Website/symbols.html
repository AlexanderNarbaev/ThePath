Symbols & Art

The visual language of The Path

The Open Spiral

❂

The Open Spiral represents growth without closure. Unlike a closed circle (completion), the spiral expands outward endlessly — symbolizing human potential and the ever-improving future we create together.

Colors

  • Dark Blue (#0F172A): Depth, wisdom, stability
  • Gold (#FBBF24): Growth, light, opportunity
  • White (#FFFFFF): Clarity, openness

Art Guidelines

  • Keep it clean and minimal
  • Use the spiral as the primary symbol
  • Prioritize clarity over complexity
  • No religious or political imagery
